About Suresh Memorial Institute

Suresh Memorial Institute, established in 2000, is a premier Bengali medium private school dedicated to providing quality education with a holistic approach. We blend traditional values with modern teaching methodologies to nurture the leaders of tomorrow.


Our campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, ensuring that every student has access to the best resources for their academic and personal growth.

Admission Procedure (2026-27)

  • Application forms are available online and at the school office.
  • Entrance tests are conducted for classes I to IX.
  • Interview with the Principal for final selection.
  • Documents required: Birth Certificate, Transfer Certificate, Marksheet.

Important Dates

  • 15 Nov Form Distribution Starts
  • 20 Dec Entrance Test (Phase 1)
  • 10 Jan Final List Declaration
